Team Eligibility Criteria
• All members of the participating team should have a valid college ID card and should produce it at the time of registration. No team member will be allowed to participate without college ID card.
• The teams can have names of their choice and should have a team logo.
• A maximum of two team members are only allowed to participate and they are only eligible for getting certificates. The team mates can be from the same college or different colleges/university. Registration
• The registration can be done in the website or on spot registration is also allowed.
• There is no registration fee for the event.
• The team can register in the official website WWW.DRESTEIN.IN. Task
• The teams will have to compete in two rounds, Knowledge round and Machining round.
Knowledge Round
• The participating teams will have to answer a set of MCQ’s provided by the organizers.
• Each MCQ carries a weightage of one mark and the organizers will decide the total number of questions.
• The teams will be ranked based on the marks scored in the knowledge round.
• If any two team’s scores are in a tie, the priority will be given to the team that has completed the round first.
• The questions will be related to the Manufacturing technology.
• The top 12 teams based on the rankings will propel to the next round.
Machining Round This round tests your practical skills.
• Every team will be provided with a cylindrical work piece of the same dimensions.
• The team’s will be handed with a Design Draft sheet which will specify the required dimensions that the cylindrical work piece to be machined to.
• The measurement tools will be provided to the team’s only if they ask for them and specify the purpose of it.
• The teams will be judged on following criteria.
Judging Criteria
S. No Category Points
1. Accuracy 150
2. Finishing 150
3. Time 100
4. Question & Answer session 100
5. Total 500
The question & answer session will be with the judging committee and the questions will be related to the machining process.
Event rules and regulations
• If the lathe machine is found damaged during the event by the team member, the team will need to pay for the damage.
• If any team is found not abiding by the rules, then the respective team will be eliminated from the event.
• The decision made by the judges and the organising committee is final; any further arguments by the participants will lead to the termination of the entire team from the event.
• Tobacco products and alcohol is strictly banned inside the college premises. If any team is found using or in possession of the banned products, the team will be expelled from the event immediately.
• Judge’s decision will be treated as final and binding on all.
• The organizers reserve the rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered teams through E-mail or by phone.
