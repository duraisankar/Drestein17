Engineering Spies

Event Details :

Format :

ROUND 1:
Find The Culprit!! - A box inside which components along with other stuff will be plaed and the player should find the component just by feeling it
ROUND 2:
Make The Weapon!!
All components will be provided and circuit diagram will be given. The team is expected to connect it within five minutes. ROUNDS 3: Transmit the Password - The participants will be given a word which they have to encode in binary and send it to the other team members using torch light and the other team should decode it and find the answer

Rules and Regulations :

• Students are expected to register as a team of three members
• Students in team can be from any department
