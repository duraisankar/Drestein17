Mind Sweepers

Event Details :

Format :

Round 1: HOUSE OF CARDS
We will show a circuit in the display, the team has to select the correct card from the given clumsy of cards and should connect it properly as same as the displayed image
Round 2: C-DOKU
The team will be provided with 4 logical equations.They have to figure out all the components(logical devices) & draw them in the form of a Sudoku.
Round 3: WARFARE
An electronic device will be assigned to each team,they must speak on why that device should be saved(convince the judge) & also should answer the questions raised by other teams & justify that their opinion is correct.

Rules and Regulations :

• 3 per team
• Time limit for first round 80 secs
• Electronic gadgets are not allowed
• Final decision will be taken by the event coordinators
• The event coordinators was all the rights to disqualify a team
• Discussion with other teams is not allowed
