Event Details :

Format :

ROUND 1: TICKET TO HOUSE
Initially 20 members are selected. They are questioned based on simple circuits conducted in the manner of rapid fire round. Out of 20 only 15 members are selected and are allowed to enter the bigg boss house.
ROUND 2: LUXURY BUDGET TASK
The house inmates are split into three groups namely ,
1. amplifying team
2. rectifying team
3. modifying team
The bigg boss will announce the rules, regulations and tasks to be conducted.
TECHNICAL EVENT: Identify the circuit
NON TECHICAL EVENT: luxury budget tasks
This round carries 20 points
ROUND 3: GORILLA’S GOAL
All the team members need to perform in the gorilla’s goal task. They are split among themselves, both technical and non-technical events will be conducted by bigg boss.
TECHNICAL EVENT : circuit puzzle
NON TECHNICAL EVENT :gorilla’s goal
This round carries 40 points.
ROUND 4: TICKET TO FINALE
This round is based on freeze and release, both technical and non-technical events will be conducted by bigg boss
TECHNICAL TASK : FIND THE ERROR IN CIRCUIT
NON TECHNICAL TASK: Freeze and release
. This round carries 80 points.
ROUND 5: THE FINALE
The bigg boss event is conducted in 3 slots. Only one member is selected from each slot and final round is purely based on technical event
CIRCUIT DEBUGGING task is conducted to decide the finalists of the event.
