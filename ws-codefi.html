Workshop Details :

The workshop will deal with strategic implementation of Embedded Systems and IoT (Internet of Things), through the building of a surveillance bot that can be operated in a WiFi network. The bot will have an android smartphone. Its camera is used to transmit video. The bot is controlled via a web based application.
