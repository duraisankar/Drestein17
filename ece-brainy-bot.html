Brainy Bot

Event Details :

Format :

ROUND 1: TECH LUDO
This round is quite similar to a ludo game.
A member of each team will play the game and the player will be asked the number of questions that he throws in the dice.
For each correct answer the player will move forward one step and for each wrong answer the player will move backward one step.
ROUND 2: GAMBLE IN JUMBLE
In this round relevant words will be written in play cards and mixed with other cards.
The player from each team has to compete to collect all the cards within a given amount of time.
ROUND 3: CROSSWORD MANIA
A crossword puzzle will be provided to each team.
Each team has to decipher the words related to advanced electronics.
The teams will be given 3 minutes time to use their mobiles and start a
debate about the technology words the deciphered."

Rules and Regulations :

1. Each team should have a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members
