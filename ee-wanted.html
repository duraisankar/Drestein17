Wanted

Event Details :

Format :

Initially some of the personalities photos(whoever may be) has been cut in to 8 pieces and kept at different places(at anywhere in campus).The search of the wanted personality initiates when each team picks a piece of paper accumulated in the bowl in which the piece of paper contains a clue. The clue has the message which directs the team towards the next clue held by a person. The person gives a piece of photo out of 8 pieces and also a puzzle which has to be solved and given to the person. Then the person will handover the message that directs the team towards the next clue . This process is continued until all the 8 pieces are found. After the personality is identified, the entire details of the personality will be given. The team has to find the personality and must click a selfie and show it to the organising members. The first 3 teams which finds the personality and shows the evidence(selfie) to the organising members will be rewarded.

Rules and Regulations :

1.Each team must have 3-5 members
2.The puzzles will be completely technical and it can be in any genre i.e, crossword, problem, technical quiz, circuit debugging, etc
3.The rewards are given only to the students from external college
4.Only participation certificate will be given for our college students
