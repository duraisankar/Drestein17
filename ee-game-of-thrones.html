Game Of Thrones

Event Details :

Format :

Round 1 (DON’T BREATHE):
This round accounts for a total time of 2 minutes. The participant should hold the given ping-pong ball with the help of a straw and should drop it in a nearby bowl. If the participant can do this successfully, he or she can pick a paper from a lot which consists of various dares. Every successful dare accounts a score. Any number of dares can be done within the given time limit. The dares include Dumb-C, Connexions, Simple questions, Odd one out, etc(Technical Questions). The total score of the team decides whether the team enters the next round or not.
Round 2 (LUCKaaa KICKaaa):
The team will be given a set of three chances. The participants will be given three balls to hit 5 cups (spacing between the cups will be high) in which any of the three cups contains questions. The team which hits the cups and answers the question correctly will be credited marks. The questions will be of simple technical questions.
Round 3 (DARKNESS MATTERS):
The selected team from the previous rounds will be given a circuit to view for a couple of minutes. The main task of this round is to find the components in the given circuit and assemble the circuit. Team by team will be allowed inside a dark room which contains many paper bundles, from which a small box which contains the required components should be identified and taken within the given time limit. Then the team must assemble the circuit. The time limit decides the score of the team.
Round 4 (TWISTERS POINT):
A complicated circuit will be given. Each player of any two teams with their bots should move the bots in their given line in the circuit like a current flow. The team which comes to the output first will be the winner, if the team couldn’t reach the output within the given time, points for crossing every deadlines will be considered and the winner will be announced.

Rules and Regulations :

1. Two per team.
2. Rounds should be completed within the given time.
3. Mobile phone and calculator not allowed in the event.
4. The discussion with other teams is not allowed
